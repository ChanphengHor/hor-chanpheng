üìä Data Structure

The CV data is organized into the following sections in Firestore:

  • personal: Name, title, and summary
  • contact: Address, phone, email, website, LinkedIn
  • education: Educational background
  • development: Self-development activities
  • experience: Work experience
  • skills: Technical skills and tools
  • additionalExperience: Additional work experience
  • languages: Language proficiencies
  • references: Professional references
